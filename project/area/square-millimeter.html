Square Millimeter

Abbreviation/Symbol:
  • mm2
Unit of:
  • Area
Denifition:

A measurement of area equal to one millimeter length by one millimeter width.

The square millimeter, or square millimetre, is a multiple of the square meter, which is the SI derived unit for area. In the metric system, "milli" is the prefix for 10-3. A square millimeter is sometimes also referred to as a square mm. Square millimeters can be abbreviated as sq mm, and are also sometimes abbreviated as mm2. For example, 1 square millimeter can be written as 1 sq mm or 1 mm2.

Origin:

Area is commonly represented in the form of a square with sides of a specific unit of length, written as "square" followed by the chosen unit of length. The square millimeter is one of many such units and is derived from the area of a square with sides of one millimeter.
