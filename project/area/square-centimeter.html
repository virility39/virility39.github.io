Square Centimeters

Abbreviation/Symbol:
  • cm2
Unit of:
  • Area
Denifition

A measurement of area equal to one centimeter length by one centimeter width.

The square centimeter, or square centimetre, is a multiple of the square meter, which is the SI derived unit for area. In the metric system, "centi" is the prefix for 10-2. A square centimeter is sometimes also referred to as a square cm. Square centimeters can be abbreviated as sq cm, and are also sometimes abbreviated as cm2. For example, 1 square centimeter can be written as 1 sq cm or 1 cm2.

Origin:

Area is commonly represented in the form of a square with sides of a specific unit of length, written as "square" followed by the chosen unit of length. The square centimeter is one of many such units and is derived from the area of a square with sides of one centimeter.
